About Us
Our school foundation was set up in 2011 and it has been nine years since,We had started the noble profession of educating the toddlers. Since,2011 our school had been adopting modern techniques to make children learn things while playing which is the need of the hour. Every year the number of students are increasing and almost every children get admission in reputed school by passing the interview with flying colours.
OUR VISION:
Every kid is extraordinary and unique. As we know that no two thumbprints are similar. So do each kid which is brought into the world novel is unique. Childhood is the most fun and memorable time in anyone’s life. It’s the first stage of life which we enjoy in whatever way we like. Besides, this is the time that shapes up the future. Moreover, it’s the golden period of life in which we can teach children everything.It is time when the moral and social character of the children develop. It is rightly said that:
"Children’s are like pottery vessels whom you can shape in any way you like"
As one method of educating doesn't work for each youngster. We have to instruct the manner in which they learn, not drive them to become familiar with the manner in which we educate. Education should foster real understanding: Understanding is different from acquiring information. Schools are not sequential construction systems of schedule and tests. We help children make meaning of life and develop the muscle to lead life effectively. For this, we go beyond academics to include life skills, values and habits.
