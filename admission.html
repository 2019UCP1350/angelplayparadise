F.A.Q's
  Why should I choose your school?
As the name of our school suggest that we provide home like environment,moreover learning there is quite enjoying as we use modern teaching techniques to make your child learn while playing.
  Number of teachers and maids in the school?
We have 2 teachers and 1 maid per classroom.
  What facilities do your school provide?
We provide alot of facilities to make the stay of your little tot at school enjoying. A few of them are
  • Our school is fully air-conditioned
  • We provide filtered R.O water
  • We also provide van service
  • We also provide day-care facility
  Do you have day care service?If Yes,what are the charges?
We do provide daycare service at our school. Charges of daycare depends upon timing.
  • Rs 500 per hour per month without food.
  • Rs 1000 per hour per month with food.
  What is unquie in your school?
Although our school provide all the facilities that a Pre-School provide but one unique feature that make us different is,in order to boost the confidence level of your little tot and make them familiar with what they are going to face in actual interveiw, we organise mock interview at various schools like
  • B.C.M
  • M.G.M
  • Ram Lal Basin
It help them to pass the interview with flying colours.
  When the admissions in the school starts?
For Nursery and Pre-Nursery Class the admissions start from the month of February while for Pre-Nurserry it goes throughout the year.
  What is right age for our child to send to your school?
Although a child start learning right from the birth and age donot matters in education.But inorder to make your child adapt a new environment without you it is right to send it to our school when he/she is above 2 years old .
Rules and Regulations:
  • Parents should pay the Admission charges,Monthly fee and Bookset charges at the time of admission.
  • Monthly fee should be deposited within first 10 day of the month.
  • After due date of fee Rs 50 will be charged per day as a fine.
  • Parents must inform the school authority in advance about the person who is going to pick and drop you tot from school
  • No student will be allowed entry in the school after the timings(except in some urgent situations)
